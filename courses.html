Our City and Guilds NVQ Level 3 Beginner’s Course will equip you with all the essential skills you need to embark upon your hairdressing career; gaining exceptional knowledge within an exciting and inspiring world-famous hairdressing environment. Nurturing and encouraging our students continuously, we guide you through each element of the course to reach your fullest potential. You can do the 18 week cutting and styling or extend your education by continuing onto the 30 week programme, which incorporates 9 week intense colouring programme, plus an additional 3 weeks to get you salon ready. Combining demonstrations, seminars and workshops on live models, we will prepare you for your final graduate presentation – the ultimate culmination to showcase your skills.
