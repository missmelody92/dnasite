    About Us

    Award Winning Hair Professionals, Dale Herne and Angelo Vallillo, are the latest innovators in the industry. The pair have taught their high in demand courses all over the world including Europe and Asia. Their beliefs are, in order to be the best, it is a must you are up to date with the latest hair techniques. Whether you are an established self employed stylist or a budding entrepreneur D&A can assist you with first-class training for you and your staff. If your desire is to be your own boss, as established business owners D&A can guide you. All training will take place in D&A Artspace, a Beautiful creative hub in the heart of Nottingham City Centre.

    The Academy

    You will be taught by industry professionals Angelo and Dale. All training carried out will be in small groups.

    Angelo is a Passionate and Motivating educator, fresh with ideas and Enthusiasm. His unparalleled technical skill is married with a down-to-earth approach that ensures his seminars are Inspirational and Enlightening, yet participants never feel out of their depth.
